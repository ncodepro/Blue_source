Description

World Famous Waterfall Photos turned into Photo Frames to decorate your photos with ability to Add Text and Draw on Photos in Styles.
We licensed World Famous Waterfall Photos from top photographers to create these beautiful waterfall Frames to create best Photo Frames for your Photos.
Waterfalls Frames for Photos includes Famous waterfalls from all over the world. We worked with Famous Photographers to create these wonderful Nature Photo Frames for you.
How to Frame your photos with Waterfalls Frames for Photos:
1. Select one of the 14 world famous Waterfalls.
2. Chose the Photo you want to include the Waterfalls Frame. You can chose the shape of Photo to be Heart, Circle, Square, Star Photo.
3. Add Text on Photo or Draw on Picture free hand.
4. Save the Frames to your Photo Gallery.
Create Best looking photos with Waterfall Frames that are unique to our application. These Nature Photo Frames are not available anywhere.
Waterfall Live Wallpapers are added from Version 2.
Waterfall Live Wallpapers include 5 real animated Photo Wallpapers that be set as your Live wallpaper.
You can chose Waterfall Live Wallpaper and browser through the waterfall Wallpapers and chose the Waterfall Wallpaper that you like and set it as Waterfall Live Wallpaper.
Worlds gorgeous Waterfall Frames now support animated Waterfall Wallpapers.
Cut and Paste your Photos against Waterfalls Photo Frames using Waterfall Backgrounds options. Cut paste photo editor as part Waterfall Photo Frames is easy to use:
1. Cut the Photo where you want to extract your photo to be pasted on Water Photo Frame.
2. Using Advanced Trim you can Cut Image along the edges to make it Better.
3. Paste Photos on Waterfall Photo Backgrounds so that the Pasted Image looks gorgeous on Photo Backgrounds selected.
