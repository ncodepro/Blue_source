Description

Music Player is one of the best music player for your android phone with powerful music play control. You can manage your media files like mp3, ogg, flac, wav, m4a, mp4 easily, Music Player will help you to find all the music in your phone easily. Audio player is based on artists or albums and the folder structure. You can browse and play your music by albums, artists, genres, songs, playlists, folders, and album artists using this MP3 Player.The format supported by Music Player are mp3, ogg, flac, wav, m4a, mp4 (provided that your phone supports these formats).
Features
• Browse and play your mp3, ogg, flac, wav, m4a, mp4 files by albums, artists, genres, songs, playlists, folders, and album artists.
• Allows folder selection.
• Choice of 5 different home screen WIDGETS (4x1,2x1, 2x2, 3x3, 4x4, 4x2).
• LOCK SCREEN widgets supported, no necessary open the app to control your music play now.
• Notification STATUS support: display album artwork, title and artist, play/pause, skip forward and stop CONTROLS (ICS only) in notification status.
• SHAKE IT feature: give your phone a shake to play next/previous song.
• Audio Player allows to create playlist SHORTCUTS.
• Music Library wide SEARCH.
• Cover art animation.
• High level of customization via settings.
• Tag editor.
• Headset/Bluetooth Controls
