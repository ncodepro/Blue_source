Photo Mixer Editor is one of the easier yet powerful collage making tools in the android market. This
amazing app allows you to make grid mixer photo of your favourite images with vivid shapes on bright &

Photo Mixer Editor App Feature ::==
* Simply select and load photos from mobile gallery or Camera to the app.
* Create beautiful collages quickly & easily on your phone.
* You can add text, adjust the font size or set the colour of the text as you like.
* You can also resize text by just your finger and fit to your screen.
* Resizing photos is quite simple. Rotate, & Zoom in or Zoom out.
