Description

DJ Music Equalizer lets you adjust sound effect levels, so that you get the best out of your Music Player. Apply Equalizer Presets based on Music Genre, or quickly create your own custom preset with the 5 band Equalizer controller.
What's new:
1. Quick notification access: use the notification to quickly change the current equalization or access the app
2. Works with all music player applications
Features:
* Virtualizer effect
* Bass boost effect
* 9 equalizer presets
* Save custom presets
* Media volume control
* Five band equalizer
* Can turn off and turn on equalizer effect
Note: Long press the "close" button to close the equalizer
