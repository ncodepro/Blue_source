Description

This app increases the efficiency of your android's RAM by killing the background apps that are active. Often we need to run an complex app or a big game on your low RAM devices like 1 GB or 512 MB RAM devices, & it hangs or shows sluggish behaviour. This app solves this problem, you need use it before running the heavy game. Free RAM also increases the battery life
This app kills more RAM than manually doing by going into your device settings, Manage App, stopping running processes, or killing each of the app one by one. This way you end with wasting so much time & also the amount or RAM cleaned is less. This app cleans more RAM than your previous method and all the unnecessary background tasks are also cleaned by this app making the maximum available RAM memory.
It is because we do not want to confuse users by making a lot of complex layers & modules which are unnecessary like many other apps out there in the market does.
