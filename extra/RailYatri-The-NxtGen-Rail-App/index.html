Description

RailYatri app tells you PNR Status, train running status live 24x7,accurate waitlist confirmation prediction & lots more.
Whether you are a daily commuter, business traveler or on a vacation, this is a must have app for indian railways travellers looking for information. Rail Yatri maintains one of the largest and most updated train database of Indian railways. All reserved, unreserved and suburban trains on indian railway are covered in the application.
--------------------------------
Also available - Kolkata Local, Mumbai Local, Ahmedabad Local Trains, Amritsar Local Trains, Chennai Local Trains, Delhi Local Trains, Hyderabad Local Trains, Jalandhar Local Trains, Katra Local Trains, Pune Local Trains & Vijayawada Local Trains.
--------------------------------
Highlights of the Rail Yatri App
==================================
1. GPS based live Train enquiry - Running status of any train anytime. Share trips with your friends via whatsapp and/or track them as they enjoy their travel.
2. PNR Status Predict – PNR Status Predictor predicts the future status of your Wait listed PNR based on the history of over 15 lakh PNR numbers. Proprietary mathematical algorithm with tested accuracy. Save money by not buying multiple / tatkal tickets.
3. Time Tables - Works offline to save your data costs, Check on-time ratings of trains at your station to help you better decide on a train.
4. Platform numbers - No more entering from the wrong side of the station.
5. Seat availability- Check which WL has the best chance of confirmation. Make informed buying decisions.
6. Coach positions, Seat Layout and berth layout for your upcoming journey on Indian Railway train coaches.
7. Manage your trips – PNR numbers from IRCTC SMS added automatically!
8. Fare enquiry and fare calculator for Indian rail travelers. Check the break-up of travel and meal-wise break-up of catering charges.
9. Local trains - Regular travelers can save their route once. Check the next upcoming trains from the home page itself. Search less and do more!
10. Lowest battery & data consumption - Thoroughly tested on-track by our tech team for the things that matter to you.
